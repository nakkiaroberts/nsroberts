My Journey to Software Development

I had a taste of programming while in college as a Computer Science major and absolutely loved it, but journey was unfortunately, quite short lived at that time.

My journey "back" to Software Development began many years later as I was searching for something different as a career choice. I enrolled in a boot camp and realized how much I enjoyed seeing the logistics of web development. I love to see how things work, the behind the scenes of things. Python and JavaScript were my first languages. While the journey at that time was arduous, I knew I had to keep going. I had to learn more.

My learning never stopped after that first experience. Along came my latest endeavor into the world of software development. I have added more languages and frameworks to my tool belt. While it has been challenging, I am truly looking forward to wherever this road leads next!
